Savings

We save not because we want to, but because we have to - By Harry Bett

The idea of Saving vs Investment is an age old argument that goes back thousands of years. Well, maybe not that far back.
However, knowing when to save and when to invest had proved troublesome for a lot of people. Let me tell you more about Saving to put your mind at ease.
With the recent pandemic, it brought to test peoples' financial stability. Having savings that can be easily withdrawn and used often proved helpful given that some lost their jobs.
This is not aimed at scaring you into saving but is meant to inform you of the benefits of having a Saving culture no matter how small.
So start saving today and slowly watch as the seeds of your future financial stability start to grow.

  1. Educational Savings

    2. When it comes to education, you hope that you or your family will have enough to propel them to the next level.

  2. Retirement Savings

  3. Health Savings

    4. When it comes to your health it is always wise to play it safe. Having a fall back plan, should things go south, is always a safe bet.
