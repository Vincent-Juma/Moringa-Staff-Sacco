Who We Are
MS SACCO is a licensed Deposit taking Savings and Credit Co-operative Society regulated by Sacco Societies Regulatory Authority. MS SACCO was registered in 2021. MS Sacco prides itself in its core purpose, which is to create wealth for its members through innovative savings products and provision of low interest credit facilities. What are our core values? MS Sacco is driven by the following values:

Integrity: We shall always maintain high standards of integrity. Equity: We shall always accord equitable treatment to all our members. Innovation: We shall consistently come up with innovative solutions. Passion: We are passionate about our members. Accountability: We shall always operate in transparent manner. Efficiency: We shall strive to deliver services efficiently.
